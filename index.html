About Us

We're working on building a safer space for investors in decentralized finance by partnering with legitimate communities with a proven track record of supporting real projects on the Binance Smart Chain and Ethereum blockchain.

Our Vision

The future of DeFi revolves around innovation and creativity. We hope to create a space where investors can participate in projects while having peace of mind about their investments. The ideal world is one where the industry and developers have access to the widest possible markets while being able to provide credible technological assurances that their projects are secure.

Our Mission

The mission at NotRugInu is to create security and project solutions while innovating in DeFi with the goal of providing safety, security and transparency to both crypto investors and project developers.
